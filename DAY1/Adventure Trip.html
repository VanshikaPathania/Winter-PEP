Places we visited

DAY 1 AND 2

Triund is a hill station and popular trekking destination in the Dhauladhar mountain range of Himachal Pradesh, India

  • Trekking: Hike the 9 km trail to Triund, which takes about 4 - 6 hours
  • Camping: Spend the night at a camp at the top of triund
  • Mesmerising Sunset and Sunrise: that was definitely the best part

DAY 3 AND 4

This small yet profound city of Uttrakhand is popularly known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World'. The sacred Ganga River, with its crystal-clear waters, weaves through the heart of Rishikesh

River Water rafting in Rishikesh was a heart-pounding adventure. we experienced the rush of conquering exhilarating rapids and witnessing the mesmerizing natural beauty of the Himalayan foothills.
